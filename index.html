Our Team's Goal:

Forge is a group of Purdue students with a variety of majors and interests. With diverse backgrounds and variable skill sets, we have all come together to collaboratively ideate on the creation of various computer graphics projects.


Currently, we have been contracted by the Swedish company SAAB, a corporation striving to serve the global market with world-leading products, services, and solutions from military defence to civil security. Our job is to create a simple and effective safety video for incoming guests that will access the more dangerous areas of their main facility.


Together, we will work hard in order to go above and beyond and . . .

Forge Ahead
